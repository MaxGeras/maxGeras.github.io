Germany

Berlin is the capital city of Germany.

The German military system for reporting casualties was based on a numerical reporting
of casualties by individual units and a separate listing of the names of individual
casualties. The system was not uniform because various military branches such as
the Army, Air Force, Navy, Waffen SS and the military hospitals each had different
systems of reporting. In early 1945 the German High Command (OKW)
prepared a summary of total losses up to January 31, 1945.
